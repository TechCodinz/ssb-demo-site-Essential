No. SSB is a tool, not a money printer. We provide AI-powered signals and filters, but crypto trading is inherently risky. You control your settings, risk, and capital. Never trade money you can't afford to lose.
What's the difference between Cloud and Local?
Cloud plans run on our servers 24/7, include Telegram alerts, remote monitoring, and auto-restart. Local EXE runs on your computer—no cloud features, but one-time payment and full control.

Yes. All licenses are tied to your email + HWID + token. Cloud plans validate every 5 minutes. Sharing your license will result in automatic suspension. We take security seriously.

Absolutely! You can upgrade anytime by paying the difference in price. Contact support via Telegram with your current license email, and we'll handle the upgrade.

USDT (TRC20) only. We use the TRON network for fast, low-fee transactions. Send to our official wallet and submit your TX hash for verification. No credit cards or PayPal.

Crypto payments are final and non-refundable. That's why we offer a FREE DRY RUN demo—test the engine risk-free before buying. If you have technical issues, contact support.
How do I contact support?
Telegram is fastest: @SSB_Support. For license issues, include your email and TX hash. We typically respond within 1-6 hours.
What RPC nodes should I use?
We recommend Helius, Quicknode, or Alchemy for best performance. Cloud plans include our optimized RPC pool. Local users need to provide their own RPC endpoint.
Does SSB work on Mac/Linux?
The EXE is Windows-only. However, we provide Python source code that works on Mac, Linux, and VPS. Cloud plans work on any device via web dashboard.
What is the AI Risk Engine?
Our proprietary engine scores each token 0-100 based on liquidity depth, volume spikes, holder patterns, honeypot detection, whale activity, and more. Only tokens passing your confidence threshold trigger trades.
